### 12/28/2016
```

>I haven't seen your son at the playground.

>Yeah, He is under the weather. He's got cold.

이런, 안됐네요.
>Oh, I'm sorry hear to that

다행히도, 감기가 그렇게 심하지는 않아요.
>thankfully, His cold is not that serious

병원에 데리고 가셨어요?
>Did you take him to a doctor

아니요, 그냥 감기가 떨어지기를 기다리고 있어요.
>No, I just waiting for go away

>have cold, have got cold

>I have a cold
```
